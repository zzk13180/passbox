Introducing Passbox - the password management software that guarantees complete privacy and security!
With Passbox, you can store your sensitive data without worrying about data breaches or unauthorized access. Passbox is open source, secure, and free, and it runs locally on your system without the need for internet connectivity.
Passbox uses AES encryption algorithm to safeguard your data, which is then saved in a local file for easy access. The initial password is randomly generated and stored in a local file, but we recommend setting up a unique password to ensure maximum security.
